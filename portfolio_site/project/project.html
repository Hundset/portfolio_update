Compact Carbon Capture - Exam 2018

Compact Carbon Capture is a company in need of a website to help them sell their product; "groundbreaking new technology for CO2 capture". My approach was to try and give the site a profile that would capture the company "personality" and the purpose of the product simultaneously.

This gave me two interesting contrasts to work with; the contrast between their more conservative, "practical" bearing and demeanor, and the modern and progressive nature of their technology.

Considering these values, the classic combination of blue and orange as complimentary colours seemed appropriate.

Blue is a colour that has a universally calming effect on us; inspiring feelings of safety and trust. Orange on the other hand, is far more energetic and optimistic, and I chose this to reflect the ambitious desire of spreading a more practical, cost-effective solution for CO2 capture.

Orange is also a fairly industrial colour, which also inspired the more silver-hued heading; another colour that will be seen upon most all security gear and garb.

The choice of font was made to reflect much the same properties as I meant to communicate with my colour palette: functional but with a modern aesthetic.

The client was dealing with an international target audience, so I decided for a minimalistic design, which lead me to decide for large background images as a visual element, particularly on the home and technology page.

Since the product itself aims to protect the environment, a clear blue sky seemed appropriate, and a horizon of clouds was intended as a symbol for keeping said skies clear.

Since blue was such a dominant element, I saw fit to provide a call to action: the orange button, directing the user to learn more about the product.
